- [Getting Past Procastination | Rahul Pandey via IEEE Spectrum (June 2025)](https://spectrum.ieee.org/getting-past-procastination) - "Action leads to motivation, not the other way around." Start with something small that will make progress towards your goal. Making progress will make you feel better and (hopefully) spur you on to continue with the task at hand
- [Have Bad Days and Let Others Have Them Too | Stay SaaSy (January 2021)](https://staysaasy.com/management/2021/01/16/Bad-Days.html) - How you act on a bad day can have consequences, especially if you are in a leadership role. Reschedule any high stake commitments, be upfront about your situation, and let others have bad days too
